ABOUT ME

My name is Jessica Martin, a Content Creator based out of Canada. As a digital media creator, I have a passion for creativity, design, and innovation. As a highly organized and detail-oriented professional with excellent communication and project management skills, I bring a unique blend of talents to the table.

I graduated from Humber College School of Media Studies and Information Technology with a Bachelor Degree in Digital Communications. Throughout my program, I gained experience with digital media from various multi-media projects, digital storytelling, web application and development, and social media marketing. I am proficient in 2D design software including Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop and InDesign, as well as 3D art design and animation using Adobe After Effects and Blender. Through these techniques, I was appreciative of expanding my skills in the real world and gaining new experiences along the way. I worked as a Design Intern at a start-up printing company, Paper Crane, which taught me the dynamics between company and client. I had the pleasure of diversifying my skills as an Intern at Humber Radio, learning the ins and outs of the entertainment industry and connecting with people and the stories along the way. I am always open to new projects and believe I would be an asset to any company looking for a well-rounded team member with the eagerness to learn.
