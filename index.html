School Life

W.P. relies on the 44 Kipling South bus or 188 Kipling South Rocket bus to get to Humber College Lakeshore Campus. He avoids rush hour to avoid sharing the bus with rowdy high school students from Lakeshore Collegiate Institute and Father John Redmon Catholic Secondary School. He is currently enrolled in Humber’s Human Resources Management postgraduate program and usually commutes to the nearby Cineplex Cinemas Queensway.

Volunteer Life

On Tuesday nights, W.P. travels across Toronto to coach his men’s basketball team. Although W.P. is knowledgeable about the sport, he is not athletically talented enough to make an impact on the court. Instead, he manages a group of former college and university varsity players and lives vicariously through their success. The commute is tough, because he often has to take the 101 Downsview Park bus that comes only every 20 minutes and stops running at 10 o’clock in the evening.

His Free Time

W.P. enjoys spending his evenings and weekends with his girlfriend. They enjoy trying out new restaurants downtown and attend Raptors games frequently. Kensington Market and the Eaton Centre are also favourite choices for the couple, who take the 501 streetcar/bus back and forth because it conveniently connects to the 44 Kipling South, 188 Kipling South Rocket, 76A Royal York South, and 110C Islington South buses.

His Job

W.P. works at Cineplex Cinemas Queensway, a large movie theatre that attracts hundreds of customers on weekdays and thousands on weekends. He is a seasoned employee who works the dual roles of a trainer and supervisor. Over the years, W.P. has created a great name for himself among management and frontline staff alike. He is cross-trained at almost every position in the building and is always willing to jump into assist others and share his knowledge.

His Home Life

W.P. lives near Bloor West Village and has the luxury of living right by a bus stop. He usually takes the 79A to Runnymede Station and takes the westbound subway to Islington Station for work or Kipling Station for classes at Humber Lakeshore Campus. At home, W.P. likes to watch sports or binge watch new shows on Netflix. He also stays over frequently at his girlfriend’s place near the Lakeshore Campus.
