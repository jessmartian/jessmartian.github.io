Mountain Mist

I thought it would be a challenge to find and incorporate certain tones and hues from this image that would be intriguing and complementary to a website. A viewer may look at this image and perceive it to be dark, gloomy and depressing, however, with the hex values I selected there is a variety of hues and tones to be used. These values are not just black and white, but instead the dark blues and light greys that are complementary to earthy tones, or in this case, the mountain. I understand a website should be easy to follow but also engaging to a viewer to keep them interested, and by using the lightest hues as the background and the darkest shades as the font or link colours it would be a good contrast to help the viewers read. As for the darkers greys, they would be a nice contrast as a header, using the light hues as the font colour. I envisioned the website to show this image of the mountain as a dimmed banner with the title of the website in large font across the banner. The title of the website would be in a light hue of grey so it would be easy to read. Once scrolling down to different sections of the website, the background would be the lightest hue selected from the colour scheme, with the text being the darkest hex value with whatever complementary images that follow the colour scheme.
